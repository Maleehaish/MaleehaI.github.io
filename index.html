Yaqoob Jewellers

Logo and print design

Yaqoob Jewellers asked me to rebrand their current logo and produce something elegant yet stylish and intricate. Inspired by their exquisite jewellery, I incorporated the intricacy of their designs into the new logo, ensuring it reflected the brand's sophistication and quality.

Design Approach:

Inspiration: I drew inspiration from the delicate patterns and details found in Yaqoob Jewellers' jewellery. This included examining their best-selling items and understanding the elements that symbolize luxury and refinement.

Typography: For the typography, I chose a font that complements the intricate design while ensuring readability. The Saravasti style adds a touch of classic sophistication, aligning with the brand's high-end image.

Color Palette: The color palette was carefully selected to reflect the opulence of Yaqoob Jewellers. The deep blue symbolizes the brilliance of diamonds and the black conveys elegance and sophistication. The blue accentuated the luxurious nature of their jewellery, while the black was used for typography and elegant patterns, creating a striking contrast and enhancing the overall design.

Gorly Pop

Icon set for user interface

“Gorly Pop,” an upcoming cosmetic store, asked me to create a collection of icons that meet their user needs and look aesthetically pleasing on their application. The theme was hearts, and they requested the icons to be cute but also clear in their purpose to ensure easy navigation throughout the application. This project allowed me to explore and play with vectors, ensuring the user experience is clear and consistent while adding an artistic flair. I integrated the graphic aspect with user needs, creating a delightful yet functional interface.

In this project, I designed five essential icons for the Girly Pop application:

  • Home Page: A welcoming icon to return to the main screen.
  • Search Page: A magnifying glass with a heart for easy searching.
  • Checkout: A shopping bag for the checkout process.
  • Wishlist: A heart icon for saving favourite items.
  • User Account: A user profile icon with a heart for personal account management.

Canine Defence

Welcome to "Canine Defence," the ultimate virtual reality game designed to teach you the essential skills for surviving dog attacks. In this immersive training experience, you will navigate a variety of realistic scenarios, each crafted to enhance your awareness, response, and safety techniques when faced with aggressive dogs.

Harnessing the power of VR technology, "Canine Defence" provides a safe and controlled environment where you can practice life-saving strategies. From understanding canine body language to executing effective defensive manoeuvres, this game equips you with the knowledge and confidence needed to stay safe in real-life encounters.

Step into "Canine Defence" and transform from a novice to a seasoned survivor, all while enjoying a thrilling and educational adventure. Get ready to face the challenge and master the art of dog attack survival.
